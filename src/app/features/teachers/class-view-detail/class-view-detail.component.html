Course Description

This advanced mathematics course covers calculus, linear algebra, and differential equations. Students will develop problem-solving skills and mathematical reasoning through rigorous theoretical and practical applications. The course prepares students for higher education in STEM fields.

  • Understand linear algebra fundamentals and matrix operations
  • Apply differential equations to real-world problems
  • Develop critical thinking and analytical skills
